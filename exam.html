Eligibility Criteria
  • Must have passed 10+2 examination or equivalent with 45% aggregate marks in any discipline (Science, Commerce and Arts) with mathematics as one of the subject.

Selection
Procedure
  • The selection will be based on an entrance examination followed by counseling.
  • The entrance examination will be of 90 minutes and will comprise 50 Multiple Choice/Objective type questions in two sections:
    • Mathematics: 25 questions
    • Mathematics: 25 questions
  • The cut-off for the entrance examination will be decided by the university.
  • The shortlisted candidates will be called for counseling, where they will be interviewed by a panel of experts.
  • The final selection will be based on the candidate's performance in the entrance examination and counseling.
